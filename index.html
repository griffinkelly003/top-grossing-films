Disney adventure films are the most financially successful films of all time

by Griffin Kelly

New York -- Though films like Bong Joon-ho's "Parasite" receive praise and awards for its contributions to cinema, it's blockbuster adventure films that have the most success financially.

The top ten highest-grossing films of all time typically involve big stakes -- Thanos is looking to wipe out half the universe or the Empire has another Death Star. "Avengers: Endgame" sits at the top with roughly $2.8 billion at the box office.

Spencer Harrison of the Harvard Business Review says Marvel movies are like puzzels in which "moviegoers become active participants within a larger experience."

"Marvel systematically builds anticipation for its coming films by putting 'Easter eggs' in its current releases that suggest a future product without giving away the story. The most obvious example is its famous post-credits scenes."

Disney owns eight of the top-grossing films. However, James Cameron's "Avatar" and "Titanic" were acquired after Disney purchased 21st Century Fox studios in 2019.
